FAQ

Common questions about contacting our team

These quick answers help explain what to send, how mixed-service enquiries work and how you can reach us fastest.

How can I request a site visit in Dubai?

You can request a site visit by calling, sending a WhatsApp message, or using the contact form on this page with your service type, location and project details.

What details should I send before asking for a quote?

Area size, site photos, floor condition, preferred material, location and timeline all help us respond faster and guide you more accurately.

Can one enquiry include more than one service?

Yes. You can enquire about multiple services in one request, such as wooden flooring with tile work, stone with marble, or carpet fitting together with other finishing requirements.

Do you work on homes, offices and hospitality spaces?

Yes. We work on villas, apartments, offices, restaurants, hotels, shops and other residential or commercial interiors across Dubai.

Can I contact you directly on WhatsApp?

Yes. WhatsApp is available for faster communication if you want to share service details, photos, location information and project notes directly.
